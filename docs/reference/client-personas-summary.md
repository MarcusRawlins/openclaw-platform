# Persona 1: The Discerning Bride üóΩ

## Quick Snapshot
**NYC energy. Editorial + documentary. Wants legacy imagery that feels effortless and elevated.**

## Demographics
- **Age:** 26-38
- **Location:** East Coast, metropolitan areas
- **Career:** Career-driven, often advanced degree
- **Wedding Budget:** $250k-$500k+
- **Photography Budget:** $15k+

## Psychographics
- Sophisticated and well-traveled
- Appreciates architecture, fashion, art
- Emotionally intelligent but guarded
- Values legacy over trend
- Sets tone rather than follows it

## Emotional Journey

### Before Booking
**State:** Excited but cautious  
**Fear:** "Will they understand my aesthetic without over-directing me?"  
**Need:** Someone who gets her vision instinctively

### After Receiving Gallery
**State:** Soft. Emotional. Undone in the best way.  
**Feeling:** She feels seen. She feels beautiful. She feels like herself.

**Behavior:** She watches the gallery with her husband. They cry. Then she immediately shares it with family, bridesmaids, and friends.

## Core Desires
- Magazine-level imagery without a staged feeling
- Efficient portrait time (respects her time)
- Complete trust in the creative process
- Heirloom albums across multiple households

## Objections & Questions
- "Are they elevated enough for my planner's level?"
- "Will they blend seamlessly with my creative team?"
- "Do they understand my aesthetic without over-explaining?"
- "Will this feel natural for my fianc√©?"

## What Closes Her
‚úÖ Seeing herself reflected in the portfolio  
‚úÖ Calm, confident communication  
‚úÖ Evidence of refined detail work  
‚úÖ Relaxed grooms in your content
