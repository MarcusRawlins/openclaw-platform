# Devotd.com - Product Analysis (from screenshots)

## What Devotd Actually Is

A passion-based social platform where fans journal, connect, and transact around their "obsessions." Starting with theater/Broadway.

## Core Features (Observed)

### User-Facing
1. **Feed** - Theater-focused social posts. Users post under specific show tags (Hamilton, Les Mis, Wicked, The Outsiders, Back to the Future). Like, comment, share, save.
2. **Moments** - Experience journaling. Log shows you've attended with date, venue, show name, who you went with. Multiple views: Grid, Wall, Carousel, Timeline, Map.
3. **Obsessions** - Follow specific shows, teams, restaurants, entertainment. Already has categories beyond theater: Sports (NFL teams), Dining. This is the multi-vertical architecture.
4. **Deals** - Discounted tickets/offers (e.g., "80% off Hamilton" with promo code)
5. **Medallions** - Gamification/achievement system
6. **Saved** - Bookmark content

### Admin/Backend
7. **Ads system** - Create/schedule/track ads. Currently 1 active ad (Take It From the Top, Birmingham).
8. **Deals management** - Create deals with promo codes, track views/claims
9. **News pipeline** - 3-step automation: Scrape News → Match Articles → Generate Posts. Currently 0 articles (not activated yet).
10. **Merchandise** - Printful integration connected. Print-on-demand merch. 1 product, 0 orders, $0 revenue.
11. **User management** - 21 total users
12. **Kanban** - Project management built in
13. **Notifications system**
